An Overview of PC's I've built

As I've gotten older, I've had the chance to save up some money and finally take on the task of building a PC for the first time. For my very first build, I took a total of 12 hours building, and about 2 days of troubleshooting. Fast forward to today, I've built 4 PC's throughout the year, and I hope to build more.
